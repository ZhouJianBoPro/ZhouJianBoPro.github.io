1. windows下载安装openssl
    > https://oomake.com/download/openssl

2. 调出PowerShell窗口
    > 进入openssl安装目录，C:\dev\OpenSSL-Win64\bin，按住shift+鼠标右键调出PowerShell窗口

3. 生成私钥
    > ./openssl genrsa -out private.txt

4. 基于私钥生成对应公钥
    > ./openssl rsa -in private.txt -out public.txt -pubout
